Computes the matrix-vector product for complex-valued and transposed matrix and vector.

Compute the matrix-vector product: result=(matrix)^T*vector

For matrix having dimension MxN, vector must be of dimension M and result must be of dimension N.

Note that in contrast to ifx_mat_mul_cv the matrix in the multiplication is transposed.
